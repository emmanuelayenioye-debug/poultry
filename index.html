About Us

Founded in 2015, Ogo-oluwa Farms has grown from a humble startup into one of Ekiti's leading suppliers of poultry and farm produce. We believe in sustainable farming, transparency, and delivering value to our customers. Our team includes experienced farmers, animal scientists, and customer service professionals who are passionate about agriculture and feeding the nation with quality food.
